Biography

Feb, 2015

Tianyang Li, grew up in Liaoning, China. She attended Dalian University of Technology (DLUT) in 2009 and majored in Electronics and Information Engineering (English Intensive). She was a Mitacs Globalink intern in Mechanics Department at the University of Victoria for three monthes in summer of 2013. She was working on the project titled "Haptics Communication through Computer Networks". In 2014, she received her Bachelor Degree of Engineering from DLUT.

She is a M.A.Sc of Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Victoria from 2014 fall. Currently, she is working on ECG project with her group members and responsible for QRS detection and Signal processing. Besides, she is also interested in Digital Signal Processing, Web Development, Android App development and Network Programming.
